Class descriptions

Tango Level 1:
START HERE. If this is your first time trying tango, then this is the class for you. No experience & no partner needed. Simply show up! In this class, you will learn the fundamentals of argentine tango, such as the embrace, the roles within the couple and their interaction. Basic movements will be introduced to get you dancing from day 1. Become a part of this magical dance!
Once you feel acquainted with the contents of this course, you’ll be ready to step up to Tango ½.
Tango Level ½:
Beginners' lesson for those with little to no experience. If you have already taken your first steps in our school or others, but intermediate classes seem too far out of your reach yet, then this is the place for you. If this is your first time and can’t make it to Tango 1, you are also welcome to start here but expect a rather challenging class.
In Tango ½ we continue consolidating the fundamentals while building up your comprehension of the dance and your repertoire of movements. The goal of this course is that you can confidently execute basic sequences without interrupting the flow of the dance.
Enjoy the results of your hard work soon enough, stepping up to Tango 3.

Tango Level 3:
At this level, we help you become a confident social dancer. We will cover the elements that show up in the milonga as well as different combinations between them. You will also adventure into different dynamics and qualities, focusing on clean execution and strong communication with your partner.
To be able to follow this class comfortably and to be a suitable partner for your classmates to practice with, It is important that you have spent enough time in the beginner courses (Tango 1 and Tango ½). Some experience and understanding of the fundamentals are required.
Milonga Level 3:
Make milonga your favorite tanda! Develop your rhythm and speed, in cadency with the music. Learn how to make your movements smaller and precise. Add playfulness and variety to your dance. Bring your tango skills to this style and discover a whole new array of sensations and emotions to enjoy in your dance.

Tango Level 4:
In this class, we begin to go deeper into the details of your dancing. Knowledge on most elements is expected in this level, so that quality may be the main focus. We will work on changing music dynamics, combining elements and giving meaning to the movements we choose. We will also aim at improving the communication within the couple, understanding how we can inspire our partner and contribute to the couple’s creativity.
Tango Alternativo Level ⅘ :
Let's do tango, but break a few rules. This is the class in which the limits of tango are stretched. We will explore movements and music that might not fit the traditional pista, in the search for more expressivity and new ways to connect. Intermediate to advanced level is required to partake in this class.

Tango Level 5:
Experienced tangueras and tangueros are welcome here to enhance their sensitivity and subtlety. In this class, we want to help you discover and build your own tango, connect to why you choose this dance and learn how to dance from this or any true motivation. To achieve that, we will develop expressivity tools related to sensations, colors, textures, dynamics and different aspects of quality.
Expect more attention to detail, as well as 'out of the box' movements or inspirations. Get ready to take your technique and your improvisation skills to the next level.

All Levels:
Develop your individual technique. Train solo and achieve greater control of your body mechanics in all sorts of tango movements and dynamics. We will help you become more free and empowered, as well as a more generous partner to dance with.
Among other faculties, you will improve your body awareness, understand what and where your axis is, and learn to connect your parts together for any given tango movement. Dancers of all levels are encouraged to partake in this class.

One Private Lesson:
Experience personalized and full attention with a one hour one-on-one private lesson for 800 SEK. Get specific instructions tailored to your unique needs and achieve your goals faster with the help of your teacher.
Normal Package:
Achieve your goals in a 3-hour long process with a set of 3 private lessons for 1900 SEK with the same teacher. Benefit from follow up from class to class and see the progress you've made in each lesson.
Combo Package:
Improve your skills from different perspectives and practice with different bodies in a 3-hour long process with a combo package of 3 private lessons for 2000 SEK with a combination of teachers. Set your goals and pursue them with the help of teachers who combine forces to ensure you achieve them.
