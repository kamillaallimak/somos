Class description

We learn simple steps and practice how and when to use them with music. The main focus is dancing in an open embrace, practicing walking, ochos, the cross, different tempos, cortado.

We work on creating a bigger vocabulary, with more technique to make it more comfortable and fluid. Focus is ganchos, sacadas, musicality, volcadas, colgadas, waltz, improvisation.

Having a better technique enables you to be strong, keep your balance and connect within your own body when dancing. Our goal is to make you feel strong, stable, confident and beautiful.

Training your technique together with a teacher, or your partner, practicing connection in yourself and with your partner, with a broader repertoar, playing with dynamics and rythmics.
