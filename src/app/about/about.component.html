About us

Somos Tango Stockholm was born many years ago in the hearts of four friends and colleagues who shared a vision in Buenos Aires. Sara, Juampi, Adrián and Dulce dreamed of creating a space in which tango could be spread as the treasure it is: an exquisite form of art, a healing practice, a socializing activity, a life-changing passion. Based on the values of respect, professionalism and kindness, Somos Tango Stockholm opens its doors to every person in the community who wishes to discover the fantastic world of Argentine Tango. Our goal is to provide a safe and fun environment where you can learn your first steps and get familiar with the tango culture.

We also strive to be a school where dancers can develop their skills to the highest level. Get ready to learn the technique and the knowledge that will help you reach the high quality dance that you’re craving for, in an efficient and organic way. Come join us, and let’s tango together!
